Open Technology App is an item, task, and workflow management platform built entirely on the open-source stack covered in this blog. It runs on Next.js and Supabase, deploys to Vercel, and every organization gets its own fully isolated database and subdomain.

## Features

Projects, queues, boards, dashboards, automations, and time tracking — all in one deployment.

<img src="/ota-features.svg" alt="Open Technology App Features" style={{width: '100%', borderRadius: '12px', margin: '1.5rem 0'}} />

- **Organize** — Projects, queues, and custom fields for every item your team tracks
- **See your work** — Table view or Kanban board; drag cards to update status instantly
- **Dashboards** — Stat cards, bar charts, and progress bars to track what matters
- **Automations** — Triggers, field changes, and webhooks to Slack, Zapier, or any endpoint
- **Time tracking** — Log time per item; see actual vs estimate with no extra tools
- **Public portal** — Embeddable intake forms with no account needed to submit

## Access Control

Role-based access enforced server-side. A demoted user's next request is rejected immediately — no restart, no delay.

<img src="/ota-roles.svg" alt="Open Technology App Role-Based Access Control" style={{width: '100%', borderRadius: '12px', margin: '1.5rem 0'}} />

- **Admin** — Manage users, all settings, automations, full data access, read/write items, comments, time logging
- **Member** — Read/write items, comments, time logging, full project access
- **Viewer** — Read all items, time logging, public comments
